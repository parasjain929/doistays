Our Story

Rooted in Kumaon

Doi Stays was born from a simple love for the Kumaon hills — their quiet mornings, the sound of rivers cutting through valleys, and the unhurried rhythm of mountain life.

We restore and run properties that honour the land they sit on. No concrete boxes, no loud tourism. Just thoughtfully kept spaces where you can slow down, read a book by the fire, walk through oak forests, and remember what stillness feels like.

Each property is personally looked after. The food is local. The pace is yours.

The Experience

What Awaits You

Himalayan Views

Wake up to panoramic views of snow-capped peaks. Watch the light shift across the mountains through the day — every hour a different painting.

Slow Living

No itineraries. No rush. Read by the fireplace, nap in the afternoon sun, stargaze at night. Time moves differently in the mountains.

Riverside Walks

Follow trails that wind along mountain rivers. The sound of flowing water, birdsong, and rustling oak leaves — nature's own soundtrack.

Local Food & Chai

Home-cooked Kumaoni meals made with local ingredients. And endless cups of chai on the veranda, watching clouds drift through the valley.
